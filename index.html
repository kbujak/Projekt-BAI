Project description

The main goal of this application is to allows the user to chat with people under a specific topic. In addition, it enables anonymity, which allows people to share their experiences and views in a completely free manner.

Users have the option of creating chat rooms, joining them and running private conversations.
Additionaly there has been implemented functionality of finding people nearby, which allows the user to find people with similar or the same interests who are close to the user's location. We believe that thanks to this functionality, we will make it easier to group the people with similar hobbies and points of view.

Active chats preview

Users can actively control the list of chat rooms they are currently participating in - both private and group:


Status change

There is possibility to change availability status for one of three options: active, busy and do not disturb.


Looking for people around

In order to provide our users with the best possible experience and enable them to find people with similar interests as possible, the search functionality of people with similar or similar interests near the current location has been implemented.

After clicking "log out" button from navigation bar user is being singed out from his temporary or registered account and moved to loging in page.
